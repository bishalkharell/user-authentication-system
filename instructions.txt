
1. Configure Settings for Sending Email Notification after Registering

    Go to emailSender/Settings and configure with requirements...

    EMAIL_HOST = ' Your Email Host EG: GMAIL, HOTMAIl'
    EMAIL_PORT = HOST EMAIL PORT HERE
    EMAIL_HOST_USER = 'FROM: your email '
    EMAIL_HOST_PASSWORD = 'Password : EMAIL Password'  
    EMAIL_USE_TLS = True / False
    EMAIL_USE_SSL = True / False

    # It is optional
    # EMAIL_BACKEND = 'django.core.mail.backends.console.EmailBackend'

2. Views configure
    Visit auth_users/views --> send_email() function
    Replace 'youremail@something.com' with original Email
    It also can be modified depending on requirements
